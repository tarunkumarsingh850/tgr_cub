Day-to-Day Inventory Forecast Analysis

Easiest way to create day-to-day forecast report for your inventory

Features:

  • Creates a day-to-day forecast report for your inventory
  • Also supported weekly and monthly forecast report for your inventory
  • Knows when the stocks are coming and releasing
  • Flexible rows and columns information with Product, Product Category, Internal Reference, Transaction Type, Reference, Location, Warehouse and Date
  • Compatible with Odoo 14.0 Community and Enterprise Editions

Access Inventory Forecast Analysis

From Menu

First, the user must be in Inventory / Manager group. Then go to Inventory > Reporting > Inventory Forecast Analysis.

Then Inventory Forecast Analysis will be shown with days in column, products in row, and quantity and commulative quantity as measures. The rows and columns can be changed with Product, Product Category, Internal Reference, Transaction Type, Reference, Location, Warehouse and/or Date.

From Product

Go to Inventory > Master Data > Products (or any Products menu).

Select the product you want to check the inventory forecast.

Click the Forecast Analysis header information.

Then Inventory Forecast Analysis will be shown with days in column, warehouses in row, and quantity and commulative quantity as measures. The rows and columns can be changed as well if needed.
